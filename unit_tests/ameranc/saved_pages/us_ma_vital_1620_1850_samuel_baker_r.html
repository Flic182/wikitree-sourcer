https://www.americanancestors.org/DB190/rd/14010/925/253509394

Description

At the turn of the twentieth century NEHGS was instrumental in introducing and passing legislation to appropriate funds to produce books of vital statistics to the year 1850 for the cities and towns in the Commonwealth of Massachusetts. This Massachusetts Vital Records to 1850 database contains all of those towns and many others treated by other authors and organizations. Not all Massachusetts towns are included. Records start at the time of the Mayflower landing in 1620

Each volume in this database has a different source, which is often a book or manuscript in the NEHGS library from a variety of provenances. We've created a Source Citation PDF which matches each volume to its source material, to help researchers understand exactly where the information comes from.

For all amended records from 1841 to 1920, please see: Massachusetts: Vital Records, 1916-1920.

Keywords: Hint, Hints, Hinting, Mass VR, MassVR

Search Tips

This database supports the following searchable fields:

  • First and last name
  • Year
  • Record type
  • Location
  • Keywords – for additional record information

Wild card characters are available for name fields. Helpful symbols to use in your name search include:

  • ? to match any single character
  • * to match any number of characters - Note that two characters must precede the * wildcard.

Description

Index to the population schedules listing the inhabitants of the United States for the year 1920. The 1920 federal census lists the names of every individual residing in a household as well as their relationship to the head of the household. This census also provides information about an individual’s birthplace, marital status, occupation, and the birthplace of his or her parents. If foreign born, this census also provides information about an individual’s year of immigration and naturalization status.

Keywords: MACensus, 10MillionNames

Search Tips

This database supports the following searchable fields:

  • First and Last name
  • Location

All records are of type Census, and the date is the census year.

Wild card characters are available for name fields. Helpful symbols to use in your name search include:

  • ? to match any single character
  • * to match any number of characters - Note that two characters must precede the * wildcard.

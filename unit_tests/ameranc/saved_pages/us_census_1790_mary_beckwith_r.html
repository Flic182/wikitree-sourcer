Description

Name index to the population schedules listing the inhabitants of the United States in 1790. This was the first national census conducted in the United States. No schedules are known to exist for Delaware, Georgia, Kentucky, New Jersey, Tennessee, and Virginia.

This index was supplied to NEHGS by FamilySearch.org. Page images are available for viewing at Ancestry.com for those that have an Ancestry.com membership.

Keywords: MACensus , 10MillionNames

Search Tips

This index-only database supports the following searchable fields:

  • First and Last name
  • Location

All records are of type Census, and the date is the census year.

Wild card characters are available for name fields. Helpful symbols to use in your name search include:

  • ? to match any single character
  • * to match any number of characters - Note that two characters must precede the * wildcard.

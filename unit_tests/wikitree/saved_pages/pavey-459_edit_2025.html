How to Add Sources

Citing sources is in our Honor Code. Add them below the bio like this:

== Sources ==
<references />
* Pavey, John. The Big Pavey Book. Chicago, IL: Pavey Publishing, 1914.

The Pavey farm was 20 acres.
<ref>

</ref>

How to Add Categories

Categories group profiles by location, cemetery, military unit, etc. ... and enable you to see your relationships to everyone in the group with a My Connections click.

Biography

Guillaume Saulnier was born on August 23, 1906 in New Brunswick, Canada, son of Maxime Saulnier (1882– ) and Magdeleine Bastarache (1885– ).[1]

William married Jane Bettez (born in 1910; daughter of Charles C (Bettez) Bettey and Nellie Jacques). Their son was William Charles Saulnier (1936–1999).

William registered for the draft for World War II. [2]

William died on May 6, 2005 in Gardner, Worcester, Massachusetts, United States, aged 98. He was buried in Notre Dame Cemetery, Gardner, Worcester County, Massachusetts.[3]

Biography

Jean Dalgarno was born about 1790 in Maryton Scotland, she was the daughter of Alexander Dalgarno and Elizabeth Hill.

Jean Dalgairns married Alexander Livie on the 4th of February 1813 in Forfar Scotland [1]

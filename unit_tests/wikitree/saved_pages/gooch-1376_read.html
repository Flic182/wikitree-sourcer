Biography

John came from Virginia to Harrison Co, Kentucky. His wife was named Elizabeth.

Children:

  1. Thomas m. Ann Bowen Crosthwait
  2. James, m. "Nelly" Juett/Jewett[1][2]
  3. Jane "Jenny" m. William Campbell

In Oct 1795, Thomas Gooch was appointed administrator of the estate of John Gooch. [3]

Research Notes

There are only two men named John Gooch in the "1790 census" Virginia, one from Amelia Co., the other from Albemarle Co. * https://archive.org/stream/headsoffamiliesa00nort#page/82/mode/2up/search/Gooch

A John Gooch in Albemarle Co. paid taxes in multiple years, adjacent to Rowland Gooch. Ex. 1782 and 1790. John Gooch sold land to Rowland Gooch in 1791, and to Henry Harris in 1801 after he was deceased. In both, his wife was called Elleanor.

The John Gooch from Amelia Co. married Judith Radford on 20 Nov 1769. He ended up in NC according to family trees, so this is not likely him.

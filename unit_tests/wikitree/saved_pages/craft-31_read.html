Biography

Kenneth Roger Craft was the son of Thomas Craft and Sarah Britt. He was born in Stone Mountain, DeKalb County, Georgia, on 28 Feb 1954 at the "little white hospital on the hill," Stone Mountain General.

He attended Lilburn Elementary and graduated from Berkmar High School (Lilburn, Gwinnett, GA) in 1973.

Kenny married Ruby Albea in 1978 and they had two daughters, in addition to Ruby's son from her first marriage.

Kenny died on 27 March 2025 in Lawrenceville, Gwinnett County, Georgia.
